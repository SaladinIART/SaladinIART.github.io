Muhamad Solehuddin bin Muhamad Yusuf is a systems engineer focused on industrial automation,
IIoT architecture, and practical digital transformation for manufacturing environments.

My work sits between operational technology and software systems. I enjoy turning machine data,
industrial protocols, and control logic into platforms that are reliable enough for real operations
and clear enough for teams to extend over time.

## What I focus on

- Industrial automation and PLC integration
- IIoT telemetry pipelines using Modbus TCP, MQTT, and Python services
- Edge-to-cloud data architecture for monitoring and analytics
- Containerized deployment patterns with Docker
- Grafana dashboards and time-series data platforms
- Documentation-first engineering that supports handover and scale

## How I work

I favor architectures that are practical, reproducible, and easy to reason about. That usually means:

- clear service boundaries
- structured telemetry contracts
- resilience features such as retries and recoverable ingestion flows
- readable documentation that explains both the system and the operating assumptions

## Current direction

I am expanding my portfolio toward industrial platform engineering, robotics-adjacent infrastructure,
and intelligent manufacturing systems. Long term, I want to keep building technology that improves
productivity and human capability in industrial environments under the Salbotics Solutions brand.
