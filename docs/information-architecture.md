# Salbotics.uk Information Architecture

Last updated: 2026-05-10

## Site Purpose

`salbotics.uk` should work for two audiences without creating conflict:

- Recruiters and hiring managers evaluating Muhamad Solehuddin for industrial, IIoT, automation, software, or digital transformation roles.
- SME and factory buyers evaluating Salbotics Solutions for scoped consulting sprints.

Primary CTA: contact Muhamad Solehuddin for interview, role, contract, or scoped freelance discussion.

## Home

Audience: mixed first-time visitor.

Primary job:

- Establish Salbotics Solutions as practical digital transformation for factories and small businesses.
- Establish Muhamad Solehuddin as a Freelance IIoT Consultant who is open to full-time, contract, and freelance opportunities.

Recommended sections:

- Hero: brand, positioning, primary CTA to Contact, secondary CTA to Proof.
- Short credibility strip: industrial systems, IIoT, dashboards, workflow digitization.
- Two-path visitor routing: "For businesses" and "For recruiters".
- Quick proof: NEXUS demo, factory energy monitoring, documented delivery style.
- Future roadmap note: SOL is a planned operations layer, not currently sold.

Primary CTA: "Contact for interview or scoped work".

Avoid:

- Leading with SOL as a product.
- Making the site feel like a SaaS launch.
- Sounding like a generic web agency.

## Services

Audience: SME owners, factory managers, operations leads, small service businesses.

Primary job:

- Show what can be bought now without implying a broad software product exists.

Visible offers:

| Offer | Buyer | Outcome | Price range |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Factory Energy Visibility Sprint | Factories, workshops, plants | Machine/zone energy visibility, dashboard, baseline cost map, next-step roadmap | RM 2,500-6,000 |
| SME Ops Digitization Sprint | SMEs using WhatsApp, paper, spreadsheets | Intake flow, tracker, reporting view, written handover | RM 800-2,500 |
| Starter Website + Lead Funnel | Small service businesses, freelancers, early ventures | Brochure site, clear offer page, email/LinkedIn/contact path, handover | RM 500-1,500 |

Recommended sections:

- Offer overview.
- Three offer blocks with fit, deliverables, timeline, and typical range.
- Working style: discovery, scope memo, sprint, handover, follow-up.
- Boundaries: no unnecessary software, no vague transformation promises, no unsupported compliance guarantees.
- CTA to Contact.

Avoid:

- Showing the full six-offer internal menu.
- Offering RepairOS, SOL, or MES-ERP as ready products.
- Legal, tax, compliance, or guaranteed savings claims.

- Factory Energy Monitor: real-world energy monitoring lineage and rebuilt portfolio proof.
- Edge Telemetry / supporting projects: optional secondary capability proof.
- SOL future roadmap: small section only, clearly labelled "planned".

Recommended sections:

- Flagship proof: NEXUS screenshot and architecture summary.
- Real-world proof: factory energy visibility experience.
- Engineering depth: Modbus, MQTT, TimescaleDB, FastAPI, Svelte, Grafana, Docker, CI/CD.

Primary CTA: "Review profile" or "Contact".

Avoid:

- Claiming SOL/MES-ERP is production-ready.
- Overloading the page with every old portfolio item.

## Global Copy Rules

Use:

- Freelance IIoT Consultant
- practical digital transformation
- industrial systems
- scoped sprints
- proof assets
- future roadmap

Avoid:

- "available SaaS"
- "enterprise ERP replacement" as a current claim
- "guaranteed savings"
- "certified compliance"

## Acceptance Checklist

- A recruiter can understand role fit in under one minute.
- A business owner can identify the three current services without seeing internal roadmap clutter.
- NEXUS and Factory Energy Monitor appear as proof, not as confusing product menus.
- SOL/MES-ERP appears only as future roadmap.
- Contact path is email + LinkedIn only.
